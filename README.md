Dobi-III is the third and the last plane from Dobi line of aircraft designed by Lithuanian aviator [Jurgis DobkeviÄius](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Jurgis_Dobkevi%C4%8Dius). On June 8, 1926 it crashed at Kaunas Aerodrome killing its designer.
